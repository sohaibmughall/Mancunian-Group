FAQs

DO I HAVE TO PAY VAT (VALUE ADDED TAX)?

20% VAT is only charged to UK customers only. Customers from outside the United Kingdom (including USA) will not be charged VAT but you maybe charged VAT by your local customs authority on arrival in your respective country.

DO YOU DELIVER TO MY COUNTRY?

Yes, we ship worldwide using Express service and all Countries we ship to can be found here.

I AM HAVING PROBLEMS LOGGING INTO MY ACCOUNT

It sounds obvious, but make sure you are entering your details in the correct case first (lower/upper). If you have done this and are still having problems, you can re-set your password using the forgotten your password link.
Or, please do get in touch with a member of our customer service team on Emailinfo@parisian.co.uk

DO YOU ONLY SELL IN PACKS?

Yes and all Styles come in packs of 4-8 pieces per pack as confirmed for each style on the product pages. Unfortunately, we are unable to split these.

HOW DO I TRACK MY ORDER?

All orders are tracked with either Parcel Force or DHL, depending on your delivery address. Once you place your order you will receive an order confirmation email, followed by your order dispatch email once dispatched - which will contain your delivery tracking information. You can log in to your account on the website, click on Order History and your order number to find your order tracking number and a link to Parcel Force website or click here to Parcel Force to add your Tracking Number found in your Email Order Dispatch Confirmation or click here for DHL.

HOW DO I MAKE CHANGES TO MY DELIVERY AND BILLING ADDRESS?

You can change your billing and shipping address while placing an order, during the checkout process. Remember please note Billing and Delivery must be in the same country. In case of any other changes such as VAT registration number - send all the necessary updates to: Emailinfo@parisian.co.uk

DO YOU SELL PLUS SIZES?

We do not currently stock plus sizes but please get in touch with us if this is something you would like to see us sell in the future.

DO YOU DROP SHIP?

No. We do not offer a drop shipping service.

CAN I PRE-SELL YOUR ITEMS?

We do not advise you sell any items you do not physically have in your possession.

DO YOU SEND OUT SAMPLES?

No. We do not send out samples, but you can visit our Manchester showroom to see our products. Our UK showroom information can be found here.

WHAT IS THE RETURNS POLICY?

Please visit our returns policy page here. You can download a copy of the printable returns form here and you will also have one in your parcel. Or you can download and fill in the returns form excel file here and email is to Emailinfo@parisian.co.uk

CAN I RETURN SALE ITEMS?

For Sale items, we will not accept any returns due to the nature of these styles being reduced. See our full returns policy click here
